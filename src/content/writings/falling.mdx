I recently stopped speaking to my mother. She got the rest of the family in the divorce.

When I stopped, a piano began falling towards my head. The pianist asks why I'm such a bitch to the tune of "It's a Small World". Why-are-you-a-fuck-ing-bitch? Why-are-you-a-fuck-ing-bitch?

I go to work. I take improv classes. The second it is quiet, I hear the piano again.

I feel sick. I remind myself I am not, and I spit up my food.

I talk more than I used to. I read **Sharp Objects**. I take a photo of it with the hold slip bearing my name, then hide it under another book with the back facing up.

I look in the mirror and laugh hysterically. I have no teeth. I hadn't noticed before.

When I tell someone what I have done, my mouth is cotton. My mother slept on the floor of my college dorm so I could have the bed. My mother bought me separate presents for my birthday and Christmas. No, I might not speak to her ever again.

If I fall now, beneath me is nothing but air.
